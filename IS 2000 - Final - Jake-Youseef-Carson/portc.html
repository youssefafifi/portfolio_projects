Hello! My name is Carson Freeman!

I am one of the three owners of Cedar Careers. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a masters in business and a major in psychology. I was born and raised in Henderson, Nevada, but then moved to Cedar City, UT with my family. I have a wife and 4 kids who are the center of my life. In my free time I enjoy any kind of sport, hiking, and cooking, among many other things.

As an owner, I deal mainly in the psychological aspect of you finding the perfect career! I have assisted many in overcoming their fears and worries when creating their perfect resume and portfolio. Then searching and applying for your ideal job is a whole other beast that I am perfectly equipped to help you fight. I am a proud receiver of UC Berkeley’s “Top Academic Achiever Award” and the “Exceptional Career Services Award.”

I am prepared to expend every effort to ensure all tasks are completed with top efficiency and effectiveness. Adept at communicating and eager to leave every customer with a positive experience. Always ready to showcase excellence in attitude and work ethic. I have served in a variety of leadership roles from which springs many skills and talents that will prove to be beneficial to my clients.
