Welcome

Welcome to Cedar Careers. Our mission is to promote the success of Cedar City individuals and alumni through face-to-face and virtual methods. We serve the Cedar City community through individual appointments as well as presentations, career fairs, employer infosessions, etiquette events.

Our mission is to match those who are searching for employment with a job matches their interests and skills.

We intend to maintain a one hundred percent job placement rate among our applicants.


The Cedar Careers website is an important resource that you can use to help you with your job search. This Career Center can help you to find a job, to learn about different careers, and to find information about job fairs and other events.
